Our Journey

Our Story

We Yone Pot was born from the resilient spirit of Sierra Leonean market women, youth groups, and families who have used Osusu for generations to build their futures together.

We witnessed firsthand how traditional cash-based groups, while rooted in trust and community, often struggled with manual record-keeping, security risks, and lack of transparency. A contribution could be forgotten, a payout delayed, and members left without recourse.

That's when we asked ourselves: "Why can't we protect these hard-earned savings with modern technology while preserving the cultural values that make Osusu special?"

Today, We Yone Pot bridges the gap between tradition and innovation. By digitizing the contribution and payout cycle, we're not just moving money—we're helping our users build a digital financial identity that opens doors to broader banking opportunities while keeping the community at the heart of everything we do.
